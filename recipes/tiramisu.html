Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is an iconic Italian dessert renowned for its luxurious and harmonious blend of flavors and textures. This elegant dessert features layers of delicate ladyfingers soaked in espresso and coffee liqueur, alternating with a luscious mixture of mascarpone cheese, egg yolks, and sugar. The mascarpone filling is creamy and subtly sweet, while the coffee-soaked ladyfingers provide a satisfying contrast. A dusting of cocoa powder on top adds a touch of bitterness that balances the sweetness, resulting in a delightful coffee-infused dessert that's both creamy and airy, making it a classic choice for indulgence.


Ingredients

  • Egg yolks
  • Granulated sugar
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Heavy cream
  • Espresso coffee
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Whip egg yolks and sugar until creamy.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese.
  3. Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks and fold it into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Combine espresso and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish.
  5. Quickly dip ladyfingers into the espresso mixture and layer them in a serving dish.
  6. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  7. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  8. Dust the top with cocoa powder.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  10. Serve chilled and enjoy your tiramisu!
